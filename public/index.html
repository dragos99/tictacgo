Your journey to mastery begins here

On each turn you take, you make a move in a square on one of the small grids. The highlighted square shows the last move that was taken.
When you get three knots or crosses in a row on a small grid, you have won that grid
In order to win the game, you need to win three small grids on either of the rows, columns or diagonals
But there is one very important catch! You don’t get to choose on which of the nine small grids you play on. That’s determined by your opponent’s previous move. Whichever square he picks determines the small grid you must play in next turn. The highlighted squares are the available moves on that turn.

There are some special cases thought. Moves can't be placed inside small grids that have already been won. Therefore, all the free squares of the entire board are available for the next turn.
